⚾ Dugout Score

Privacy Policy — Effective April 12, 2026

Summary

Dugout Score does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter into the app stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

Data Storage

All data you enter — including team names, player names, jersey numbers, player ages, game scores, and statistics — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never transmitted over the internet
  • Is never sent to our servers or any third-party servers
  • Is never shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any other parties
  • Is accessible only to you on the device where you use the app

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically, Dugout Score does not collect:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data

Third-Party Services

Dugout Score does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not contain any ads.

The app loads Google Fonts (Oswald and Courier Prime) for typography. This requires a single network request to Google's font servers on first load. Google's privacy policy regarding web fonts is available at policies.google.com/privacy. The app functions fully without these fonts if the network is unavailable.

Export and Backup

The app includes an export feature that saves your data as a JSON file to your device. This file is created and stored locally — it is not uploaded to any server. You control what you do with exported files. If you choose to share a backup file (via email, messaging, or cloud storage), you are responsible for the privacy of that shared data.

Children's Privacy

While Dugout Score is designed to score youth baseball games, the app is intended to be operated by parents and coaches — not by children directly. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Player names and ages entered into the app are stored only on the device and are never transmitted externally.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual games using the delete button on the home screen
  • Removing individual players from the roster
  • Clearing your browser's or app's local storage/website data through your device settings
  • Deleting the app from your device, which removes all associated local storage data

Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your data is protected by your device's own security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, encryption). We recommend keeping your device secured with a passcode or biometric lock.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Since the app does not collect data, we have no way to notify you of changes directly — we encourage you to review this page periodically.
